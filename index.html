About Me

Hello, my name is Andrew Weihe. I am a 24 year old student at Vanderbilt's Coding Bootcamp. I was born in Cincinnati, OH but grew up just outside of Detroit. I have been living in the greater Nashville area ever since the early 2000's.

I originally started school pursuing a degree in Accounting at Western Kentucky University. After coming to the conclusion of not wanting to be stuck at a desk crunching numbers all day long, I decided to move back home and figure things out and start work full-time.

I have held many different occupations within many different fields, but I still had not found something I was passionate about until now. Thankfully so, I had some good mentors to conversate with and brainstorm about my future with my father and grandpa, both of which whom were CS majors.

Ultimately, after careful deliberation, our conversations led me into the computer programming field. Web Development, and Web Design are something I truly enjoy creating and learning more about everyday.

That ultimately led me to want to tinker and learn more about how I can be more efficient and automate tasks.
